 👥 IBM HR Analytics Dashboard

File: `IBM_HR_Dashboard.pbix`
Dataset: IBM HR Employee Attrition (1,470 employees)

🔗 Live Dashboard: [View Interactive Report]([Internetut]
URL=https://app.powerbi.com/groups/me/reports/93db92ea-48f4-4475-92d4-886008446fba?pbi_source=desktopShortc)

Overview
A 3-page interactive dashboard analyzing employee
attrition patterns across departments, job roles,
compensation bands, and tenure.

 Pages
1. Overview — Total employees, attrition rate,
   attrition by department and job role
2. Employee Analysis — Income vs attrition,
   department spend, years at company vs attrition
3. Key Insights— Executive summary with
   findings and recommendations

DAX Measures Used
- Total Employees, Active Employees
- Attrition Count & Attrition Rate %
- Average Monthly Income
- Age Group classification (`SWITCH(TRUE())`)

 Key Findings
- Attrition rate of 16.1% exceeds the
  healthy benchmark of 10%
- R&D department has the highest attrition
  at 56% of total departures
- Attrition spikes at Year 1 of employment,
  pointing to onboarding gaps
- Employees who left earned comparable or
  higher monthly income than those who stayed
  in several roles — compensation is not the
  only driver





 🚗 Cars Sales Dashboard — Exodus Experts

File: `Cars_Dashboard.pbix`
Dataset: Top Cars Database — Africa (Botswana & South Africa)
🔗 Live Dashboard: [View Interactive Report]([Internetut]
URL=https://app.powerbi.com/groups/me/reports/93db92ea-48f4-4475-92d4-886008446fba?pbi_source=desktopShortc)

 Overview
A branded, dark-themed sales dashboard analyzing
vehicle sales performance across two African markets,
including an AI-generated narrative summary.

 Visuals
- Revenue, Profit, Dealer & Model count KPI cards
- Revenue by Country (donut chart)
- Total by Model (column chart)
- Total & Profit by Segment (combo chart)
- Revenue by City (bar chart)
- AI narrative insight panel

 Key Findings
- South Africa dominates revenue at 67.42%
  vs Botswana at 32.58%
- 3 Series is the top-selling model with
  26.83% of total sales
- Gaborone leads all cities at $2.01M**
  in revenue — nearly double Cape Town
- Sedan segment shows the largest gap
  between profit and total sales
